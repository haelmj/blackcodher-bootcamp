Suggested Reading

Found yourself mesmerised by the Harry Potter film series?



I have also suggested two books by Toni Adeyemi a brilliant author who tells the story of life when the magic was alive. When the help of the Orisha was so close, contact was forbidden. Told through the eyes of a young girl and her friends, you hear of:

  • Pain
  • Fear
  • Courage
  • love of family, friends, life, belief and most importantly self!
  • Spirituality

All these and more as the story unfolds within a world so close to spirituality we once knew and was taken away from us forever.

Children of Blood and Bone

Book one in the series

by Toni Adeyemi
Children of Virtue and Vengeance

Book two in the series.

By Toni Adeyemi
